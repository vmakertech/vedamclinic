Dr. Richa singh

Gynaecologist and Laparoscopic Surgeon

Welcome to the profile of Dr. Richa Hatila Singh, a highly experienced Consultant Gynaecologist in Varanasi. With over 14 years of dedicated practice, Dr. Singh is committed to providing comprehensive and compassionate care to women of all ages. Her areas of expertise include gynaecology, obstetrics, laparoscopic surgery, and infertility treatment.

Dr. Richa Singh began her medical education by earning her MBBS degree from Calcutta National Medical College in 2009. She then pursued her specialization in Gynaecology and Obstetrics, completing her MS from R G Kar Medical College in 2015. She further enhanced her expertise by obtaining DNB (Obs & Gyn) from the National Board of Examination, New Delhi in 2016. Recognizing the importance of staying at the forefront of advancements in her field, she pursued a fellowship in Minimal Access Surgery from AMASI in 2019.To further enhance her skills, she gained invaluable experience during her association with Apollo Gleneagles Hospital, Kolkata.

As a trusted gynaecologist, Dr. Richa Hatila Singh is well-versed in managing a wide range of women's health issues, including menstrual disorders, reproductive health, hormonal imbalances, and gynaecological infections. She provides comprehensive prenatal and postnatal care, guiding women through every stage of pregnancy with compassion and expertise. Dr. Singh has extensive experience in managing painless normal deliveries, ensuring that expectant mothers receive the highest level of care and support throughout their labor and delivery.

In addition to her expertise in general gynaecology, Dr. Singh is a skilled laparoscopic surgeon. This minimally invasive surgical technique allows for faster recovery, smaller incisions, and reduced post-operative discomfort. Driven by a passion for helping couples achieve their dream of parenthood, she is also an infertility specialist, offering diagnostic evaluations and advanced treatment options to optimize the chances of successful conception.

Dr. Richa Hatila Singh takes pride in providing patient-centered care, focusing on open communication, empathy, and personalized treatment plans. She understands the unique needs of each patient and strives to create a comfortable and supportive environment during consultations and treatments.

If you are seeking a trusted and compassionate gynaecologist, laparoscopic surgeon, and infertility specialist in Varanasi, Dr. Richa Hatila Singh is here to guide you on your journey towards optimal gynaecological health. Schedule an appointment today and experience comprehensive care delivered with utmost professionalism and care..

Dr. Abhinay kr singh

Expert Orthopaedic Surgeon

Welcome to the profile of Dr. Abhinay Kumar Singh, a renowned Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon in Varanasi. With over 14 years of experience, Dr. Singh is committed to delivering high-quality care and ensuring the well-being of his patients. His expertise lies in treating complex trauma cases, sports injuries, and performing joint replacement surgeries.

Dr. Singh's journey in the field of orthopaedics began with his MBBS degree from Calcutta National Medical College in 2009, followed by MS Orthopaedics from Burdwan Medical College in 2014. To further enhance his skills, he completed a fellowship in Arthroscopy and Arthroplasty from Kolkata.

During his tenure at the prestigious R G Kar Medical College, Kolkata from 2016 to 2021, Dr. Singh gained extensive experience and honed his surgical skills under the guidance of renowned orthopaedic experts. He has consistently demonstrated his commitment to staying at the forefront of advancements in orthopaedics, ensuring that his patients receive the most effective and innovative treatments available.

Dr. Abhinay Kumar Singh is widely regarded as one of the best orthopaedic surgeons in the region. His patient-centric approach, combined with his expertise and dedication, has earned him a reputation for delivering exceptional care. He believes in personalized treatment plans that address the unique needs of each patient, aiming to restore mobility, alleviate pain, and enhance overall quality of life.

If you are seeking a compassionate and skilled orthopaedic surgeon in Varanasi, Dr. Abhinay Kumar Singh is here to help. Schedule a consultation today and take the first step towards your recovery.
