Our Services

We Offer The Best Quality Gyne & Ortho Services


We are offer the following services




Routine Gynecological Care:

The clinic provides routine gynecological examinations and screenings, including Pap smears, breast examinations, and pelvic exams, to promote women's overall reproductive health.






Obstetric Care:

Vedam Clinic offers prenatal care, antenatal check-ups, and expert guidance throughout pregnancy to ensure the well-being of expectant mothers and their babies.




Family Planning and Contraception:

The clinic provides various family planning options and contraceptive counseling, helping women make informed decisions regarding birth control methods.




Menopause Management:

The clinic offers specialized care and support to women experiencing menopause, addressing symptoms such as hot flashes, mood changes, and hormonal imbalances..




Gynecological Disorders:

Vedam Clinic diagnoses and treats a range of gynecological conditions, including menstrual irregularities, pelvic pain, endometriosis, polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS), ovarian cysts and tumors, uterine fibroid and urinary incontinence.




Orthopedic Consultations:

The clinic has skilled orthopedic specialists who provide comprehensive evaluations and consultations for musculoskeletal conditions and injuries.




Joint Replacement:

Vedam Clinic offers expertise in joint replacement surgeries, including hip replacement, knee replacement, and shoulder replacement, using advanced techniques and implants.




Sports Medicine:

The clinic provides specialized care for sports-related injuries, including sprains, strains, fractures, and ligament tears, with an emphasis on injury prevention and rehabilitation.




Fracture Management:

Dr Abhinay Kumar Singh at Vedam Clinic offers prompt evaluation, diagnosis, and treatment of fractures, and also complex trauma cases, ensuring optimal healing and functional recovery.




Arthritis Management:

The clinic provides personalized treatment plans for various forms of arthritis, including osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis, aiming to alleviate pain and improve joint function.
